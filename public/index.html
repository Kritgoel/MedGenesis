Our Mission

We aim to streamline the patient doctor communication and increase the efficiency at which hospitals and clinics operate while at the same time making sure that the patients recieve clear and timely diagnosis for their conditions and the problem management part thereafter.


We achieve this with the help of our Genesis AI which is the first contact a patient has with a healthcare provider, which coupled with our automated appointment assignment with the right specialist greatly enhances communication and reduces friction.

Genesis AI

Allows patients to discuss their symptoms and then makes a rough diagnosis estimating the severity of the situation, suggested course of action and the type of specialist to consult.

Automated appointments

The appointments can be booked with the specialist directly after consulting Genesis and the specialist-appointment database updates in real time reflecting the empty slots.

Positive feedback loop

The underlying model can be trained using the feedback form from doctors informing what kind of data the ai was more/less accurate, further enhancing its capablities.

Patient data privacy

The patients' medical data will only be used to train the model locally and will never leave their healthcare institution, ensuring complete confidentiality.
